Our Foundation

Born from Compassion

The Bilqees Razia Laghari Welfare Trust (BRT) was founded in 2019 by philanthropist Haji Zaffar Ali Khan Laghari. Driven by a deep empathy for the underprivileged, he established this organization using his personal resources to uplift communities that were historically denied basic human rights.

The Trust was named in loving memory of Bilqees Razia—a symbol of selfless service. We operate on a strict Zero Admin Overhead policy, ensuring every single donation directly impacts field operations across Sindh, Punjab, Balochistan, and KPK.

Emergency Flood & Disaster Relief

During the devastating floods, our teams are on the front lines in boats and life jackets, rescuing stranded families and providing emergency food, shelter, and medical aid when it's needed the most.

Surgical Eye Camps

Our highly qualified surgeons perform hundreds of free cataract surgeries in remote areas, restoring sight and bringing light back into the lives of the elderly who cannot afford treatment.

Ration & Food Distribution

No family should sleep hungry. We regularly distribute heavy ration bags containing flour, rice, oil, and essentials to thousands of impoverished families and widows across villages.

Community Support & Engagement

We sit with the community to understand their deeply rooted issues. From listening to their health concerns to setting up immediate assistance, our connection with the locals is genuine and respectful.

Collaborative Medical Camps

Partnering with organizations like the Pakistan Navy, we set up massive medical relief camps at coastlines and deep rural areas, providing top-tier medical checkups and medicines absolutely free.

Clean Water Hand Pumps

Access to safe drinking water is a fundamental right. Deep research shows our extensive "Water for Life" project has successfully installed hundreds of hand pumps in extremely arid, remote villages of Pakistan. These pumps save children from deadly water-borne diseases like cholera and typhoid, bringing joy, health, and a sustainable water source directly to their homes.
